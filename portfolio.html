Projects I Have worked upon:

1. Aluminium Can Crusher

An aluminium can crusher is not just a device that helps you easily crush cans for recycling, it is a powerful tool that has a significant impact on our environment. By reducing the size of aluminium cans, can crushers make it easier to transport and recycle them, reducing the amount of waste in landfills and oceans.

2.Demonstration of steam engine

Steam engine demonstration project is an excellent example of how the principles of thermodynamics and fluid dynamics can be applied to create a machine that converts heat energy into mechanical work. Your project consists of a boiler, cylinder, piston, and valve system that work together to create the motion that powers a machine.

Upcoming-RFID Based attendance System

An RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) based attendance system is an advanced technology that uses RFID technology to automate the process of tracking attendance. The system eliminates the need for manual attendance tracking, reducing the chances of errors and fraud.
